# React + Vite is amazing

This template provides a minimal setup to get React working in Vite with HMR and some ESLint rules.

Currently, two official plugins are available:

- [@vitejs/plugin-react](https://github.com/vitejs/vite-plugin-react/blob/main/packages/plugin-react) uses [Babel](https://babeljs.io/) for Fast Refresh
- [@vitejs/plugin-react-swc](https://github.com/vitejs/vite-plugin-react/blob/main/packages/plugin-react-swc) uses [SWC](https://swc.rs/) for Fast Refresh

## The Tale of Bao and Jumbo: An Unlikely Friendship

In the heart of a magnificent zoo, there lived a gentle giant named Jumbo the elephant and a playful panda named Bao. Despite their vastly different sizes and backgrounds, these two became the most unlikely of best friends.

Every morning, Bao would wake up early and waddle over to the fence that separated their enclosures. Jumbo, always an early riser, would be waiting with his trunk extended in greeting. Their friendship began when Bao first rolled a bamboo ball toward the fence, and Jumbo carefully picked it up with his trunk and rolled it back.

What started as a simple game of catch became a daily ritual that drew crowds of amazed visitors. Jumbo would use his incredible strength to help Bao reach the highest bamboo shoots, while Bao would entertain Jumbo with his silly tumbling tricks that never failed to make the elephant trumpet with joy.

During the hot summer days, Jumbo would spray cool water from his trunk to keep Bao comfortable, and in return, Bao would share his favorite bamboo treats with his gentle giant friend. The zookeepers were so touched by their friendship that they eventually built a special connecting area where the two could spend more time together safely.

Their friendship taught everyone who witnessed it that love and companionship know no boundaries - not size, not species, not any difference that might seem to separate us. Bao and Jumbo showed the world that the biggest hearts come in all sizes, and that true friendship is one of life's greatest treasures.

## Expanding the ESLint configuration

Check out the [TS template](https://github.com/vitejs/vite/tree/main/packages/create-vite/template-react-ts) for information on how to integrate TypeScript and [`typescript-eslint`](https://typescript-eslint.io) in your project.
